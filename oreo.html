Oreo Shake

A creamy, indulgent, and delicious Oreo shake thatâ€™s perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings!

Ingredients

  • 10 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1/2 cup whipped cream (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate syrup (optional)
  • Crushed Oreo cookies for garnish

Instructions

  1. Add Oreo cookies, vanilla ice cream, and cold milk to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the shake into a tall glass.
  4. Top with whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate syrup (optional).
  5. Sprinkle crushed Oreo cookies on top for garnish.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
