Brownie Cake

A rich, fudgy, and decadent brownie cake that's perfect for any occasion. Easy to make and impossible to resist!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt the butter and mix it with sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, and whisk until smooth.
  4. Sift in flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Mix until combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips (if using).
  6. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Let it cool before slicing and serving.
