Vegetarian Salad

A fresh, healthy, and colorful vegetarian salad packed with crunchy vegetables and a zesty dressing. Perfect for a light meal or side dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olives (optional)
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sunflower seeds or nuts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cucumber, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, carrot, red onion, olives, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Sprinkle sunflower seeds or nuts on top for added crunch.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh and healthy salad!
